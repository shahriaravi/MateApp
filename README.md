## What is Mate?

Mate is a personal finance app for Android that keeps everything on your device. No sign-ups, no servers, no subscriptions. Track your spending, manage budgets, monitor loans and subscriptions — all in a minimal, monospace-driven interface inspired by Nothing OS.

Built by [Byontriq](https://byontriq.xyz).

---

## Features

### Core
- **Multi-wallet management** — cash, bank accounts, credit cards, savings
- **Transaction tracking** — income, expenses, and wallet-to-wallet transfers
- **Custom categories** — create, reorder, color-code with 50+ icons
- **Budget tracking** — weekly or monthly, per-category or overall
- **Subscriptions** — auto-processing with smart renewal detection
- **Loans** — track money given and taken, with payment history
- **Recurring income** — salary, freelance, investments auto-deposited
- **EMI calculator** — credit card installment planning

### Design
- **Three themes** — Pure Black, Grey, and Light
- **Monospace aesthetic** — GeistMono typography throughout
- **NDot57 display font** — for numbers and headings
- **Smooth animations** — spring-based sheets, reanimated transitions

### Privacy & Security
- **100% offline** — all data stored locally via AsyncStorage
- **Biometric lock** — fingerprint or face authentication
- **Configurable auto-lock** — immediate to 10 minutes
- **Full data export/import** — Excel (.xlsx) and JSON backup
- **Zero telemetry** — no analytics, no tracking, no third-party data sharing

### Extras
- **Android home screen widgets** — Budget ring, Quick Add, Spending Insights
- **Smart notifications** — customizable reminders for bills and due dates
- **Multi-currency support** — 49 currencies with compact formatting
- **What-If calculator** — simulate financial scenarios
- **Insights dashboard** — spending patterns and trends
